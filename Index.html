Background

Our esteemed advisor, Mrs. Juby, along with the visionary founder and President, Veer Sahni bring to you The Computer Science Club! We strive to unite students passionate about computing, programming, and technology. Through the organization of workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures, we establish a vibrant platform for knowledge acquisition, creativity, and cooperation. Our ultimate goal is to foster an inclusive community that empowers members to enhance their technical prowess, engage in impactful projects, and forge enduring relationships.
