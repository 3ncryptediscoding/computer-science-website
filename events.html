Events

  1. As a community, we organize technical workshops to teach programming languages and skills to all club members.
  2. We come together to host coding competitions and hackathons, enhancing coding skills and fostering teamwork among all participants.
  3. Together, we invite industry professionals for guest speaker sessions on emerging technologies and career paths, providing valuable insights for all club member.
  4. As a community, we encourage all club members to participate in project showcases, where they can demonstrate creativity, collaboration, and knowledge sharing
  5. As a community, we arrange code reviews and feedback sessions, allowing all members to improve coding practices and learn from each other.
  6. Collectively, we host tech talks and discussions on various computer science topics to encourage critical thinking and broaden knowledge for all attendees.
  7. As a community, we engage in community outreach through free coding workshops and mentorship programs, benefiting local schools and organizations.
  8. Together, we plan visits to local tech companies, startups, and research labs, providing real-world exposure for all club members.
  9. As a community, we initiate collaborative projects where members with different skill sets can work together to solve complex problems and build innovative applications.
  10. As a community, we conduct code-a-thons to develop software solutions for social or humanitarian causes, combining our coding skills with the power of making a positive social impact.
