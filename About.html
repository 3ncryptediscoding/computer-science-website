About Us

The Computer Science Club, led by esteemed advisor Ms. Juby and visionary founder Veer Sahni, is a thriving community of programming enthusiasts.

We organize:

  • Technical workshops to teach coding skills
  • Coding competitions and hackathons
  • Guest speaker sessions with industry professionals
  • Project showcases to demonstrate member talents
  • Code review and feedback sessions
  • Tech talks and discussions on computer science topics

Our community also:

  • Provides coding workshops
  • Arranges company/lab visits for real-world exposure
  • Initiates collaborative projects to solve complex problems
  • Hosts code-a-thons to develop software for social impact

Through these diverse initiatives, we empower our members, cultivate technical expertise, foster connections, and inspire future leaders in computer science.
