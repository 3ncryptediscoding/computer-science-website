Computer Science Club

The Computer Science Club is dedicated to bringing together students interested in computing, programming, and technology. We host workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures to provide a platform for learning, innovation, and collaboration.

Our mission is to create an inclusive community where members can develop their technical skills, work on projects, and make lasting connections.
